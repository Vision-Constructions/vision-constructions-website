What We Deliver

A seamless, end-to-end solution. One multi-discipline team.

Whether supporting planned works or responding to urgent issues, we remove the hassle of coordinating multiple contractors — with in-house engineering and experienced project managers across every phase of delivery.

Infrastructure Support Marine & Water Structural & Piping Civil Construction
01 — Infrastructure Support Services

Support where the work is hard.

Concrete rectification, operational support, asset inspection, and specialist trade services tailored to operational sites. Mobilised fast, with the fleet and the crew to handle unplanned work on remote or tidal sites.

  • Planned & preventative maintenance
  • Shutdown planning & execution
  • Emergency response & rapid mobilisation
  • On-site civil works for operating facilities
  • Concrete repair & rectification
02 — Marine & Water Infrastructure

Built for tidal conditions.

Concrete structure rectification, pile repairs, protective coatings, fender repairs, and cathodic protection for marine assets. We deliver staged works that keep client operations running while we work.

  • Jetty construction & refurbishment
  • Guide pile & pile repair works
  • Wharves, boat ramps & seawalls
  • Water supply & pipeline construction
  • Remote & island community projects
03 — Structural, Mechanical & Piping

In-house fabrication. Site-installed to spec.

Structural steel fabrication and installation, mechanical works, and piping services for industrial, mining, and resources clients. All welding delivered to AS/NZS 1554 standards with full traceability.

  • Structural steel fabrication & installation
  • Mechanical installation & removal
  • Industrial pipework & modifications
  • Qualified welding to AS/NZS 1554
  • Plant integration & commissioning support
04 — Civil Construction

Earthworks to finished surface.

Bulk earthworks, road construction, drainage, concrete structures, and site clearing across public and private networks. From remote road rehabilitation to urban drainage, we deliver under programme.

  • Road construction & rehabilitation
  • Bulk earthworks & excavation
  • Drainage & stormwater infrastructure
  • Concrete structures — bridges, retaining walls, slabs
  • NDRRA flood restoration works
