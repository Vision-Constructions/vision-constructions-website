Why Vision

Always looking for good people.

Vision Constructions is a locally owned North Queensland civil contractor delivering projects across civil construction, marine infrastructure, and the resources sector. We invest in our people — with training, varied work, and a genuine safety culture.

With projects spanning Townsville, remote Queensland, and island communities, there's always interesting work on the horizon.

We Typically Hire
  • Engineers — civil, structural, or project
  • Skilled tradespeople — boilermakers, welders, fabricators, concreters
  • Plant operators — excavator, grader, roller
  • Riggers & dogmen
  • Civil labourers & trades assistants
  • Site supervisors & leading hands
  • Administration & project support
