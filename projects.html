Our Work

200+ projects delivered safely.

Civil construction, marine works, and infrastructure support projects delivered across North Queensland and remote areas — for AIMS, TRILITY, Glencore, Townsville City Council, and more.

Civil & Water Infrastructure

Sludge Lagoon Extension — TRILITY

Expansion of the Douglas Water Treatment Plant sludge lagoon, including underdrain systems, inlet and discharge pipework, concrete structures, and valve systems. Works involved connection to the existing discharge well with engulfment protection measures.

Client: TRILITY / Douglas Water Treatment Plant Location: Douglas, Townsville QLD Completed: 2024
Marine Works

Jetty Maintenance & Upgrades — AIMS

Repair and upgrade of the AIMS service pontoon at Cape Cleveland, delivered as staged maintenance to ensure continuous personnel access. Works included modifying guide roller cradles for free tidal movement and surface preparation of early paint deterioration on piles.

Client: Australian Institute of Marine Science Location: Cape Cleveland, QLD Completed: 2023
Civil Construction

STP Structural Concrete Works — AIMS

Principal contractor for full civil concrete works at the AIMS Sewage Treatment Plant expansion. Scope included footing excavation, formwork, reinforcement, plinths and nib walls, and concrete supply and placement — delivered with zero re-work and under programme.

Client: Australian Institute of Marine Science Location: Cape Cleveland, QLD Completed: 2022
Marine Works

Jetty Guide Pile Repairs — AIMS

Design, fabrication, and installation of custom access systems for guide pile repairs in a tidal environment. Works included encapsulation and environmental controls, surface preparation, and full seal welding of 316 stainless flat bars around each pile.

Client: Australian Institute of Marine Science Location: Cape Cleveland, Townsville QLD Completed: 2024
Structural & Mechanical

Chemical Dosing Skids — TRILITY

Two-stage structural and mechanical installation at the Douglas WTP lime dosing shed. Stage 1 relocated and secured four dosing skids onto a prepared FRP mesh platform; Stage 2 installed outlet, ACH supply, and service water pipework to integrate the skids into the site's dosing system.

Client: TRILITY / Douglas Water Treatment Plant Location: Douglas, Townsville QLD Completed: 2025
